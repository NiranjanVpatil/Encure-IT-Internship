About Us

WashCare™ is the professional care for your garments, apparels, accessories, fabrics, linens, upholstery & your daily dose of fashion. It is an effort to revolutionize the way laundry is perceived, processed & promoted. The idea is not just to end your daily struggle with laundry but our aim is to provide complete wardrobe management solutions. So whether it is your office formals or your regular casual or your stylish party wear or your trendy footwear, WashCare™ takes care of all.
